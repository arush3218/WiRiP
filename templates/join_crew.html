Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of writers are you looking for?

We welcome writers of all backgrounds who are passionate about hip-hop journalism. Whether you specialize in album reviews, artist profiles, industry news, or cultural commentary, we want to hear from you.

Do I need prior journalism experience?

Not necessarily. We value authentic passion and unique perspectives. If you're dedicated to producing high-quality content about hip-hop culture, we'd love to work with you.

How often will I write?

Frequency is flexible. You can contribute as much or as little as you want. Some writers publish weekly, others contribute monthly. We adapt to your schedule.

Is this a paid position?

Currently, positions are volunteer-based as we grow our platform. However, we offer exposure, professional experience, and the opportunity to build your portfolio in hip-hop journalism.

How long does the review process take?

We typically review applications within 5-7 business days. You'll hear back from us via email with next steps.
